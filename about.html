I am a front end web developer and Bootstrap specialist living in Orlando, Florida. I'm the creator of a website called Start Bootstrap which is a library of open source themes and templates for the Bootstrap framework. I personally create and maintain all of the themes available on Start Bootstrap, and they are free to download and use for any purpose!

Blackrock Digital is the web development and digital goods agency that I own and run. Under this name, I serve small to medium sized clients with front end development needs, and produce premium digital goods that are for sale on various online marketplaces.

Although I am a web developer by trade, my educational background is in business having received an MBA from the University of Central Florida.

When I am not working, you can find me practicing and teaching Capoeira, an Afro-Brazilian martial art.

If you have questions about Start Bootstrap, Blackrock Digital, or are interested in booking me for speaking engagements, feel free to contact me and I will try to get back to you as soon as possible!
